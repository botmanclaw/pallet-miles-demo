STEP 02
Stack up to 9 levers for more
Stack up to 9 levers to earn up to +150% more: offer flexible pickup and delivery windows, take the latest Must-Arrive-By date, book 7+ days ahead, ship 2+ pallets, pick up or drop off near a Warp crossdock, or keep the trip local (under 60 miles).
STEP 03
Credits unlock on delivery
Credits unlock when your shipment is delivered. They expire after 90 days. Apply them to any future Warp shipment — there's no cap on redemption. Invoices can go all the way to $0.
